How the score is calculated

For each product in our database, the algorithm computes a weighted compatibility score:

Score = Σ (criterion_weight × criterion_match)

Where criterion_match ranges from 0 (incompatible) to 1 (perfect match), and criterion_weight is adjusted dynamically based on your usage profile.

The final score is expressed as a percentage (0–100%). Products scoring above 80% are labeled "Excellent Match", 60–80% "Great Match", and below 60% "Good Match".

Dynamic weight adjustment

Weights are not static. The algorithm adjusts them based on your primary usage:

  • FPS Gaming — Weight and DPI criteria are boosted for faster, more precise movements
  • MOBA/MMO — Button count and shape criteria are prioritized for macro accessibility
  • Office / Productivity — Ergonomics and wireless freedom are weighted higher
  • Creative Work — Precision (DPI) and button customization are emphasized

Expert review layer

Beyond the algorithmic score, each recommendation includes "L'avis de l'expert" — a subjective assessment from our team based on hands-on experience. This layer adds context that pure data cannot capture, such as:

  • Hand feel — How the mouse actually feels in different grip styles
  • Build nuances — Quality of clicks, scroll wheel, and side buttons
  • Use-case tips — Practical advice like "Top for small hands" or "Best for long sessions"

Expert tips are generated based on the interaction between your profile and the product's physical characteristics.
