What is MinSp?

The reference electronic product comparison tool

Our mission

MinSp is an independent comparison tool designed to help you choose the best electronic equipment. Whether you're looking for a gaming mouse, a mechanical keyboard, or components for your PC, MinSp centralizes all information in one place.

No more hours of searching across multiple sites: on MinSp, you compare products based on their technical specifications, brands, and usage profiles, all within a clear and modern interface.

Transparency is our core value. We believe that every consumer deserves access to clear, objective information to make informed decisions. Our recommendations are based on technical analysis, not commercial partnerships.

Who is behind MinSp?

MinSp was created by technology enthusiasts passionate about helping users navigate the complex world of electronic products. Our team combines expertise in:

  • Product testing — Hands-on experience with hundreds of devices
  • Technical analysis — Deep understanding of specifications and performance metrics
  • User experience — Focus on what matters most to real users
  • Market research — Continuous monitoring of industry trends and innovations

How our algorithm works

Our AI-powered recommendation engine is not magic—it's a systematic analysis based on 15 technical criteria designed to simplify your purchase decision. Here's how it works:

  • Profile analysis — We analyze your budget, hand size, grip style, usage patterns, and preferences
  • Database matching — Our algorithm compares your profile against a comprehensive database of products
  • Weighted scoring — Each criterion is weighted based on its importance for your specific use case
  • Match calculation — Products receive a compatibility score based on how well they fit your needs
  • Personalized recommendation — The best match is presented with a clear explanation of why it was chosen

We continuously refine our algorithm based on user feedback and new product releases to ensure our recommendations remain accurate and helpful.

What we offer

  • Detailed comparison — Each product is presented with its key specifications (DPI, weight, type, shape, polling rate, etc.)
  • Structured catalog — Mice, keyboards, PC components: each category is organized for smooth navigation
  • Smart search — Filter by brand, type, features to find exactly what you need
  • Verified data — Product sheets are built from reliable sources and updated regularly
  • Free and ad-free — MinSp is a free tool, without popups or intrusive banners
