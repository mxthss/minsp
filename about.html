What is MinSp?

The reference electronic product comparison tool

Our mission

MinSp is an independent comparison tool designed to help you choose the best electronic equipment. Whether you're looking for a gaming mouse, a mechanical keyboard, or components for your PC, MinSp centralizes all information in one place.

No more hours of searching across multiple sites: on MinSp, you compare products based on their technical specifications, brands, and usage profiles, all within a clear and modern interface.

What we offer

  • Detailed comparison — Each product is presented with its key specifications (DPI, weight, type, shape, polling rate, etc.)
  • Structured catalog — Mice, keyboards, PC components: each category is organized for smooth navigation
  • Smart search — Filter by brand, type, features to find exactly what you need
  • Verified data — Product sheets are built from reliable sources and updated regularly
  • Free and ad-free — MinSp is a free tool, without popups or intrusive banners
